About

Hometown

My name is António Pedro Araújo Fraga, I was born on August 3rd, 1995 in Oporto, Portugal to my mom Isabel Fraga and dad Francisco Fraga. I grew up living in Mirandela, Portugal. I was always very interested for programming since my highschool, I've done some math programs in my TI 84 Plus, contributing a lot to make my choice about what college to attend.

School and College

I went to Escola Secundária de Mirandela for my highschool and graduated with the class of 2013. Currently I'm attending Master in Informatics and Computing Engineering at the Engineering Faculty of the University of Porto.

Sports

I've played table tennis in Clube de Ténis de Mesa de Mirandela from 6 to 19 years old. I won some titles through my "career", including several Team National Championships in 2005, 2007, 2010 and 2013 and an Iberian Championship while was representing Bragança in 2008. I've also represented University of Porto at University National Championships in 2013, 2014 and 2015.

Programming

As I mentioned to you before my first contact with the programming world was with my TI 84 Plus, I've made some games and useful programs to my classes and tests. As a programmer I'm happy to tell you that I've already launched my first Android App in Google Play Store. In 2015 I was part of a team in Porto Summer of Code programming contest taking the 2nd place with the ReachN'Do app running in Android. I'm a mobile, web and graphical computing enthusiastic, but I like to develop any kind of Software Program.

Associativism

As associativist I'm part of the AEFEUP, this is one of my greatest passions. In 2016 I was part of Queima das Fitas do Porto organization.

About

Hometown

My name is António Pedro Araújo Fraga, I was born on August 3rd, 1995 in Oporto, Portugal to my mom Isabel Fraga and dad Francisco Fraga. I grew up living in Mirandela, Portugal. I was always very interested for programming since my highschool, I've developed some math programs with a TI 84 Plus, contributing a lot to the decision of what college/course to attend.

School and College

I went to Escola Secundária de Mirandela for my highschool and graduated with the class of 2013. Currently I'm attending a Master in Informatics and Computing Engineering at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto.

Programming

As I mentioned before my first contact with the programming world was with a TI 84 Plus, I've made some games and useful programs to my classes and tests. As a programmer I'm happy to tell you that I've already launched my first Android App on Google Play Store. In 2015 I was part of a team in Porto Summer of Code programming contest taking the 2nd place with the ReachN'Do app running in Android. I'm a mobile, web and graphical computing enthusiastic, but I like to develop any kind of Software Program.

Sports

I've played table tennis at Clube de Ténis de Mesa de Mirandela from 6 to 19 years old. I won some titles through my "career", including several Team National Championships in 2005, 2007, 2010 and 2013 and a Team Iberian Championship while I was representing Bragança in 2009. I've also represented University of Porto at University National Championships in 2013, 2014 and 2015.

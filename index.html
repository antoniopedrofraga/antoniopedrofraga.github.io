Software Engineer @ Feedzai

August 2018 - August 2019

I've worked at Feedzai for 1 year, a company that analyzes big data with machine learning in order to detect financial fraud. At that time the company handled more than $3 billion in transactions per day, so everything needed to be reliable and fast. I've worked with Java, using it to improve the performance of database accesses. I've followed the best design and programming practices in order to obtain fast, consistent and scalable systems.

M.Sc. in the UK

September 2017 - August 2018

In 2017 I moved to the United Kingdom to complete my M.Sc. in Software Engineering for Technical Computing @ Cranfield University, in north of London. I've met wonderful people there who were capable of inspiring me to become a better Computer Scientist. I've completed my M.Sc. with a mark of 85/100, and with a thesis whose score was 88/100. My thesis was about postulating a method to give an approximated solution of an Non-Deterministic Polynomial problem. In brief, a graph partitioning algorithm would run to solve a minimization problem.
